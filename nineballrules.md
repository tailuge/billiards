The cue ball must contact the lowest-numbered ball first.
A legal shot requires a ball pocketed or any ball contacting a cushion.
Any ball may be pocketed.
The game is won only if the 9-ball is pocketed on a legal shot.
If the 9-ball is pocketed on a foul, it is respotted.
On any foul, the opponent gets ball-in-hand; other object balls remain pocketed.
